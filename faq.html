Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about the 2026 hackathon.

Who can participate?

Anyone over the age of 18 regardless of background! Whether you are a student, a professional, or a self-taught enthusiast, we welcome all innovators.

Is it free to join?

Yes, Synthetix Hack is completely free. We provide meals, swag, and 24/7 support for all accepted hackers.

What is the team size limit?

Teams can consist of 1 to 5 members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! We have a dedicated team-matching session on the first day.

Is it hybrid or in-person only?

It's hybrid. You can join us at our flagship venue in San Francisco or participate 100% remotely through our virtual platform.
